How to Trade It

The Rules.
Simple. Strict. Effective.

01

Activate Your Alerts — Live Your Day

You don't need to watch the chart all day. Set your Buy Alert and Sell Alert directly on TradingView and let the indicator work for you. Once your alerts are live, put your phone down and go live your life.

🔔

Reminder: Make sure your TradingView notification sound is unique and your phone is not on silent — or set a distinct vibration so you never miss a signal. When it fires, that's money calling.

02

Wait for the Candle to Close

When you receive an alert, open your chart — you'll see the signal. Do not enter yet. A buy signal is only valid if the candle closes green. A sell signal is only valid if the candle closes red. While the candle is still forming, the signal may appear and disappear as price moves — this is completely normal. Only when the candle fully closes in the correct direction is the signal confirmed and locked permanently on the chart.

⏳

The indicator does not repaint. Once the candle closes, that signal is permanently locked — it will never disappear.

03

Stop Loss Placement

Place your stop loss below the previous low (buys) or above the previous high (sells) with a 10-pip buffer ($1.00).

📍

Example: Previous low at $3,200.00 → stop loss at $3,199.00.

04

Break Even & 1:2 Target

Two steps turn signals into consistent results:

⚡

At 1:1 RR — move stop loss to entry. Trade is now risk-free.

🎯

Target is always 1:2 RR. Let it run. No exceptions.
